Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, and our food. Can't find an answer? Reach out to us directly.

Ordering

You can place single orders through Zomato or Swiggy by searching for "The Rice Heritage". For bulk or catering orders of 20+ boxes, please fill out the enquiry form on our Bulk Orders page and we will get back to you within a few hours.
For direct catering enquiries (corporate lunches, events, poojas), the minimum order is 20 meal boxes. There is no minimum for individual orders placed through Zomato or Swiggy.
For individual app-based orders, customizations may be limited to what is listed on the app. For bulk catering orders, we offer full customization — you can request specific rice varieties, adjust spice levels, and choose your preferred accompaniments (pickle, papad, raita). Just mention it in your inquiry.

Delivery

Delivery areas are determined by the Zomato and Swiggy delivery radius active on our listing. For bulk catering orders, we can arrange direct delivery to your location within Salem. Contact us to discuss logistics for outstation events.
Typical delivery time for individual orders is 30–45 minutes depending on your location and order volume. For bulk orders, we coordinate a specific delivery time slot with you in advance.
We use premium, leak-proof, eco-friendly containers made from sustainable materials. Our packaging is designed to retain heat during transit while keeping accompaniments like pickle and papad crisp and separate.

Food & Ingredients
