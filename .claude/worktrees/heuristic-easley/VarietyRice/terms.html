6 Food Safety & Allergens

We maintain the highest standards of food hygiene and preparation. All food is cooked fresh and handled in a clean, dedicated kitchen environment.

Common allergens present in our kitchen include:

  • Peanuts — present in Lemon Rice (Chitranna)
  • Sesame seeds — present in Tamarind Rice (Puliyogare)
  • Dairy — present in Curd Rice (Thayir Sadam)
If you have a known food allergy, please contact us before ordering so we can advise on safe options or prepare allergen-free batches for bulk orders.

We are a 100% pure vegetarian kitchen. No meat, fish, or eggs are used or stored on our premises.
